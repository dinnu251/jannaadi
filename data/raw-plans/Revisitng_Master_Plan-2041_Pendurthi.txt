This comprehensive developmental plan blueprint outlines the "Draft Master Plan for Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region (Proposed Landuse - 2041)" focusing specifically on the "Pendurthi Zone." The map details proposed landuse, existing and proposed infrastructure, and geographic boundaries across Visakhapatnam, Anakapalli, and Vizianagaram Districts. Key geographic areas include the VMR Boundary, District Boundaries, Mandal Boundaries, Village Boundaries, Municipal Boundaries, T.S. Ward Boundaries, T.S. Block Boundaries, and Existing Government Survey Boundaries. Within the Anakapalli District, Mandals like Anakapalli Mandal, Gajuwaka Mandal, and Bheemunipatnam Mandal are depicted, alongside specific localities such as Donkada, Pedamadaka, Aganampudi, Duvvada, Vadlapudi, Jaggarajupeta, Pakeertakya, Ungalar, Akkireddipalem, and Pedagadi. Gopalapatnam Mandal is prominently featured with Cheepuruvalasa, Gulivindada, Nimmalapalem, Sundar, Veerabhadra, and Mindin. The Vizianagaram District includes Kothavalasa Mandal and its associated Kothavalasa locality. Visakhapatnam District encompasses Pendurthi Mandal, Anandapuram Mandal, and Visakhapatnam Rural Mandal, showing areas like Chintapalli, Balighattam, Chandigundam, Bakk, and Pedagadila. Key landmarks and significant areas visible on the map or within the VMR context include Gajuwaka, Gopalapatnam, Pendurthi, Anakapalli, Kothavalasa, Anandapuram, Duvvada, Madhurawada, and Rushikonda.

The infrastructure scope is extensive, covering existing and proposed elements. Transportation infrastructure includes a network of National Highways, State Highways, Major District Roads, Other District Roads, Village Roads, and Railway Lines. Proposed transportation enhancements feature Proposed BRTS (Bus Rapid Transit System) roads, Proposed Metro lines, Proposed Elevated Corridors, and Proposed Suburban Rail lines, designed to improve regional connectivity. Existing transportation infrastructure also includes HT Power lines and HT Pylons, indicating electrical grid development. The plan designates various landuse zones: Residential Use Zones (including Existing Residential Layouts), Commercial Use Zones, Industrial Use Zones, Public & Semi-Public (PSP) Use Zones, Transportation Use Zones, Open Space / Parks / Playgrounds, Buffer Green areas, Water Bodies, Agriculture Use Zones, Protected Use Zones, Retail Use Zones, Logistic Parks, and Government Land.

Specific projects and facilities mentioned or depicted include the Bhogapuram Airport, identified by the Bhogapuram Airport & Bodapadu (BALA) Boundary. The plan illustrates major land marks, junctions, fish landing sites, railway stations, and sea ports (though sea ports are part of the general legend, not explicitly placed within the Pendurthi Zone on this map). Cultural and community infrastructure includes religious places and existing crematoriums/burial grounds. The overarching document is titled "Draft Master Plan for Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region (Proposed Landuse - 2041)," implying the involvement of the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA) in this developmental blueprint for the Pendurthi Zone. No specific developmental budgets are explicitly stated on this map.
