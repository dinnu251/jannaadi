# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Anakapalli Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA
**Zone:** Anakapalli | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**Districts:** Visakhapatnam District, Anakapalli District
**Key Mandals:** Anakapalli, Parawada, Sabbavaram, Narsipatnam, Chodavaram
**Key Areas:** Anakapalli town, Parawada Industrial Area, Marripalem, Kurmannapalem, Kondapeta, Vempadu, Vadlapudi

## Land Use Zones
- Residential zones R-1 to R-5 — Anakapalli town core (R-1/R-2 high density) transitioning to R-4/R-5 periphery
- Commercial zones C-1 to C-3 — Anakapalli town centre and NH-16 corridor
- Industrial zones I-1, I-2, I-3 — Parawada SEZ (Special Economic Zone), Anakapalli APSEZ fringe
- Ecologically Sensitive Zones (ESZ) — Sabbavaram mandal forest areas
- Agricultural zones — extensive in Sabbavaram, Narsipatnam mandals
- PSP zones — hospitals, schools, civic facilities
- Logistic Parks — Vadlapudi, Akkireddipalem
- Special Development Zones (SDZ)
- Hill Zones (HZ) protected — Eastern Ghats foothills

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- NH-16 (Visakhapatnam–Chennai): backbone passing through Anakapalli
- State Highway SH-23: Anakapalli–Narsipatnam link
- Major District Roads: Anakapalli–Marripalem–Parawada corridor
- Railway: Anakapalli Railway Station on Howrah–Chennai main line
- Proposed BRTS: Anakapalli–Gajuwaka–Visakhapatnam city corridor
- Proposed Suburban Railway: enhanced frequency Anakapalli–Duvvada–City
- Road widening: Anakapalli town internal roads (Ramavaram, Mamidilova access)

## Drainage and Water Management
- STP: Anakapalli town — Mamidilova drain outfall
- Industrial Effluent Treatment: Parawada SEZ — separate ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) mandate
- Stormwater drainage: Anakapalli town network, open drain cover slabbing
- Canal network for irrigation-cum-drainage in agricultural zones

## Water Supply
- WTP at Anakapalli — supply from Yeleru scheme (Gosthani tributary)
- Industrial water supply: dedicated pipeline for Parawada SEZ from Yerrakalava reservoir
- Service reservoirs: Anakapalli town overhead tanks

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM site: Anakapalli town boundary (non-agricultural land designated)
- Industrial waste: Parawada SEZ hazardous waste facility (separate from MSW)

## Power and Streetlights
- 220 KV / 132 KV substations: Anakapalli, Parawada (industrial power)
- HT Power lines along NH-16 corridor
- Streetlighting: NH-16 and all arterial roads in Anakapalli town

## Health and Public Facilities
- Area Hospital Anakapalli (PSP zone, proposed upgrade)
- Community Health Centres per mandal
- Police Station, Fire Station (PSP designations)
- Anakapalli Bus Terminal (Transport & Communication use zone)
- Government Offices, Court complex in PSP zone

## Education
- APSEZ vocational training centre (Industrial zone fringe)
- Engineering college cluster near Sabbavaram
- Schools in each residential zone per sector

## Key Localities
Anakapalli, Parawada, Marripalem, Kurmannapalem, Desapatrunipalem, Gandigundam, Kondapeta, Vempadu, Chinnayapalem, Ravada, Pungavaka, Daggumilli, Ramavaram, Mamidilova, Vadlapudi, Akkireddipalem, Jaggayyapalem, Kothavalasa, Narsipatnam, Gantyada, Kottapeta

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage**: Town STP and drain network; Parawada ETP for industrial areas
- **Roads**: NH-16 widening, BRTS, Suburban Railway for Anakapalli zone
- **Water**: Yeleru-fed WTP and industrial water system
- **Health**: Area Hospital upgrade and PHC per mandal
- **Garbage**: Separate MSW and hazardous waste facilities
- **Streetlights**: 132 KV substation powers the zone grid
