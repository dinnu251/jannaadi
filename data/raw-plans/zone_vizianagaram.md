# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Vizianagaram Urban Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA
**Zone:** Vizianagaram Urban | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**Districts:** Vizianagaram District, Visakhapatnam District (fringe), Anakapalli District (fringe), Alluri Sitharama District
**Key Areas:** Vizianagaram City, Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation area, Bhogapuram, Denkada, Jami, Lakkavarapukota

## Land Use Zones
- R-1 to R-5 residential zones — Vizianagaram city core (R-1 high density) to periphery
- C-1, C-2, C-3 commercial zones — city centre, NH-16 corridor
- I-1, I-2, I-3 industrial zones — Vizianagaram industrial area, Bhogapuram fringe
- Public & Semi-Public (P&SP) zones — hospitals, colleges, government
- Open Space (OS) — parks, playgrounds
- Agricultural (AGR) land — extensive in outer mandals
- Water Body (WB) areas — Vizianagaram lake system, Gosthani headwaters
- Forest (F) zones — Eastern Ghats foothills
- Ecologically Sensitive Zones (ESZ) — biodiversity protection
- Special Development Zones (SDZ) — tourism, heritage
- Hill Zones (HZ) protected
- Transport & Communication (T&C) corridors
- Green Zones, Water Body Buffers

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- NH-16 and NH-516: primary connectivity to Visakhapatnam (75 km) and north
- State Highways: Vizianagaram–Bobbili, Vizianagaram–Parvathipuram links
- Proposed BRTS: Vizianagaram–Bhogapuram–Anakapalli–Visakhapatnam corridor
- Proposed Roads: internal city bypass and ring road
- Major District Roads: city to mandal headquarters
- Vizianagaram Railway Station (Howrah–Chennai main line)
- HT Power lines, existing BRTS Roads (city level)

## Drainage and Water Management
- STP for Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation (proposed upgrade to modern STP)
- Stormwater drainage: city network along major roads
- Water body protection: Vizianagaram lake (drinking water source), Gosayi Thota Tank
- River management: Nagavali River (seasonal flow) flood protection

## Water Supply
- WTP at Vizianagaram from Nagavali River / Gosthani headwaters
- Service reservoirs across municipal wards
- Rural mandal coverage: Jal Jeevan Mission pipelines

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM site designated outside Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation boundary
- Bhogapuram Airport influence area — enhanced SWM standards

## Power and Streetlights
- 220/132 KV substation: Vizianagaram (existing, upgrade proposed)
- HT Power distribution to all municipal wards
- Streetlighting: NH-16 and city arterial roads

## Health and Public Facilities
- Government Area Hospital Vizianagaram (PSP zone, 200+ beds)
- Maharaja's Hospital (teaching hospital, PSP)
- Police Lines, Vizianagaram District Collector Office (PSP)
- Vizianagaram Railway Station (T&C zone)
- Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation boundary demarcated

## Education
- VEMU (Vikrama Simhapuri University) fringe campus
- Government Degree College, engineering colleges in PSP zones
- Schools throughout residential zones

## Key Localities
Vizianagaram City, Bhogapuram, Denkada, Jami, Lakkavarapukota, Kothavalasa, Ravada, Chintapalli, Maradam

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage**: Nagavali River flood management, STP upgrade for Municipal Corporation
- **Roads**: NH-16, BRTS corridor linking Vizianagaram to metro area
- **Water**: Nagavali River WTP and Jal Jeevan coverage
- **Health**: Area Hospital and Maharaja's Hospital in PSP zones
- **Garbage**: SWM outside municipal limits
- **Streetlights**: 220 KV substation upgrade
