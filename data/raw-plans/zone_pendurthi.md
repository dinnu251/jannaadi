# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Pendurthi Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA / GVMC
**Zone:** Pendurthi | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**GVMC Wards covered:** 87 (Kurmannapalem Part), 88 (Narava), 89 (Chandra Nagar), 90 (Vidyuth Nagar), 91 (Nethaji Veedhi), 92 (Venkatapuram), 93 (Sai Durga Nagar), 94 (Gangiredla Colony), 95 (MES Layout), 96 (Pendurthi old Village), 97 (Kranthi Nagar), 98 (Appannapalem)
**Mandals:** Pendurthi, Anandapuram, Visakhapatnam Rural, Gopalapatnam, Anakapalli, Kothavalasa (Vizianagaram Dist.)

## Land Use Zones
- Residential Use Zones — planned expansion for urban growth node
- Commercial Use Zones along major road corridors
- Industrial Use Zones — Logistic Parks designated (Duvvada, Gopalapatnam fringe)
- PSP Use Zones — hospitals, schools, civic facilities
- Open Space / Parks / Playgrounds designated per sector
- Agricultural Use Zones in rural periphery of mandal
- Protected Use Zones — forests, hills near Kothavalasa
- Government Land designation for institutional use
- Buffer Green Areas along drainage corridors

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- National Highways passing through zone (NH-16 Hyderabad–Visakhapatnam)
- Proposed BRTS extension: Pendurthi–Gajuwaka–City Core corridor
- Proposed Metro: extension to Pendurthi from city
- Proposed Elevated Corridor connecting Pendurthi to Gopalapatnam
- Proposed Suburban Railway: Kothavalasa–Duvvada–Pendurthi–City link
- Major District Roads connecting Pendurthi to Narava, Gopalapatnam
- Road widening: Pendurthi–Chintapalli road, Pendurthi–Balighattam corridor
- Bhogapuram Airport (BALA) boundary — BAIA influence zone in north

## Drainage and Water Management
- STP planned for Pendurthi zone — Narava/Gopalapatnam outfall
- Drainage channels along Pendurthi–Gopalapatnam road network
- Village-level storm water drains with UGD planned for ward 96–98
- Water body protection: tanks and reservoirs in Anandapuram fringe

## Water Supply
- WTP feeding from Yeleru scheme — pipeline to Pendurthi wards
- Overhead tanks at Pendurthi (Ward 96) and Venkatapuram (Ward 92)
- Ward-level distribution network expansion planned

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM facility at Pendurthi fringe — serves wards 88–98
- Logistic Park zone mandates waste segregation compliance

## Power and Streetlights
- HT Power lines from Duvvada substation traversing zone
- 33/11 KV substations planned at Pendurthi, Gopalapatnam
- Streetlighting along proposed BRTS corridor and elevated road

## Health and Public Facilities
- PSP health zone: Gopalapatnam Area Hospital (serving Pendurthi zone wards)
- Ward 96 (Pendurthi old Village) — PHC (Primary Health Centre) designation in PSP zone
- Fire Station and Police Station designated in PSP zones
- Religious places: existing temples and churches mapped
- Crematorium/Burial Grounds: existing mapped, new site designated

## Education
- Schools in PSP sub-zones for each ward cluster
- Technical/Vocational Institute planned in Logistic Park zone
- Higher secondary institutions along BRTS corridor nodes

## Key Localities
Pendurthi, Gopalapatnam, Duvvada, Kothavalasa, Chintapalli, Balighattam, Chandigundam, Narava, Cheepuruvalasa, Gulivindada, Nimmalapalem, Pedagadi, Akkireddipalem, Vadlapudi, Jaggarajupeta, Anandapuram, Rushikonda, Madhurawada

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage**: STP and UGD planned for wards 96–98; drainage channels to Gopalapatnam outfall
- **Roads**: BRTS, Metro, Elevated Corridor — critical for peripheral wards 90–98
- **Water**: Yeleru pipeline and overhead tanks for wards 88–98
- **Health**: Ward 96 PHC — direct plan provision for health grievances in Pendurthi
- **Garbage**: SWM facility at Pendurthi fringe covers all zone wards
- **Streetlights**: Substation expansion from Duvvada, BRTS corridor lighting
- **Education**: PSP school zones for all 12 wards in zone
