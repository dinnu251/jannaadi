# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Gajuwaka Zone
**Authority:** Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA)
**Zone:** Gajuwaka | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**GVMC Wards covered:** 71 (Auto Nagar), 72 (Chinagantyada SC Colony), 73 (China Nadupuru), 74 (Nehru Nagar), 75 (Pedagantyada), 77 (Goruduvanipalem), 78 (Steel Plant Sectors 3–11), 79 (Sivaji Nagar), 80 (Tadi)
**Mandals:** Gajuwaka, Pedagantyada, Parawada, Anakapalli, Sabbavaram

## Land Use Zones
- R-1 through R-5 residential zones — varying density; high-density (R-1) in central Gajuwaka, low-density peripheral zones
- Commercial zones C-1 (local), C-2 (district), C-3 (regional) along NH and arterial roads
- Industrial zones I-1, I-2, I-3 — extensive industrial designation around Steel Plant, Pedagantyada, Parawada
- Public & Semi-Public (PSP) zones — educational institutions, hospitals, civic facilities
- Open Space, Parks, Playgrounds, Buffer Green zones
- Agricultural and Water Body zones in periphery
- Waterfront Development zone along coastline

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- National Highway network: existing NH and proposed widening through Gajuwaka and Pedagantyada
- State Highways and Major District Roads connecting Gajuwaka to city core and Anakapalli
- Proposed BRTS (Bus Rapid Transit System) corridors through Gajuwaka
- Existing and proposed railway lines; Duvvada Railway Station connectivity
- Proposed expressways — major arterial sub-arterial grid
- Bus terminals and depots designated in PSP zones

## Drainage and Water Management
- Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) identified at multiple locations in Gajuwaka zone
- Drainage canals and main drains mapped; UGD (Underground Drainage) planned
- Water pipelines and drainage pipelines designated
- Water bodies: tanks, canals, drains protected as Water Body (WB) zones
- Buffer green strips mandated along all water bodies

## Water Supply
- Water Treatment Plants (WTP) designated within the zone
- Water supply pipelines planned to serve R-1 to R-5 zones
- Coordination with GVMC water supply network for wards 71–80

## Solid Waste Management
- Solid Waste Management Sites (SWMS) explicitly designated within zone
- Secondary collection points in residential zones per GVMC ward

## Power and Streetlights
- HT Power lines and proposed Power Substations mapped
- HT Pylon routes for transmission from Steel Plant power infrastructure
- Street lighting infrastructure aligned with proposed road network

## Health and Public Facilities
- Hospitals and healthcare facilities designated in PSP zones
- Police Stations, Fire Stations designated per sector
- Government Offices, Crematorium/Burial Grounds, Religious Places mapped
- Commercial Centers and Markets in C-1/C-2 zones

## Education
- Educational Institutions (schools, colleges) designated in PSP zones throughout zone
- Particular cluster around Pedagantyada and Auto Nagar wards

## Key Localities
Madhurawada, Rushikonda, Gajuwaka, Pedagantyada, Anakapalli, GVMC area, Mulagada, Marikavalasa, Lankelapalem, Vepagunta, Mammidilova, Yellapuvanipeta, Madhurawada, Yendada, Chepaluppada, Anandapuram, Vangali, Chinamushidivada, Kanthigundam

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage complaints** (Ward 75 Pedagantyada hotspot): STP and drainage infrastructure planned — UGD expansion targeted
- **Roads**: NH widening and BRTS corridor directly serve wards 73–79
- **Water**: WTP planned to address supply gaps in high-density R-1 zones
- **Garbage/Sanitation**: SWMS and collection routes designated
- **Streetlights**: Substation expansion covers wards 71–80
- **Health**: PSP zones include hospital designations for this cluster
