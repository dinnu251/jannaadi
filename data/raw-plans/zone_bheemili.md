# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Bheemili Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA
**Zone:** Bheemili (Bheemunipatnam) | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**Geographic scope:** Northern coastal belt — Visakhapatnam District north to Vizianagaram District border
**Key areas:** Bheemunipatnam town, Marripalem, Chinamushidivada, Revupolavaram, Akkireddipalem, Jaggayyapalem, Vedullapadu, Pudimadaka, Gosthani River mouth, Bay of Bengal coastline

## Land Use Zones
- Existing and proposed Residential zones (medium/low density — coastal town character)
- Commercial zones along NH coastal corridor
- Industrial zones (proposed, fringe areas around Parawada)
- Heritage/Tourism zone — Bheemunipatnam historical town, beach
- Bio-conservation zones — Gosthani River estuary, coastal ecosystem
- Forest land and green cover protection (large tracts)
- Agricultural zones in rural mandal areas
- Special Development Zones (SDZ) for coastal tourism
- Water body protection: Gosthani River, coastal lagoons, tanks

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- NH coastal corridor (connecting Bheemunipatnam to city, ~25 km)
- State Highway: Bheemunipatnam–Anakapalli link road
- Proposed BRTS extension to Bheemunipatnam from Madhurawada
- Proposed Suburban Railway: Bheemunipatnam–Duvvada–City link
- Major District Roads: Bheemunipatnam–Marripalem–Chinamushidivada corridor
- Village Roads network for rural connectivity
- Port access road to Bheemunipatnam fishing harbour

## Drainage and Water Management
- STP at Bheemunipatnam (coastal outfall management)
- Gosthani River flood management — embankment and drain network
- Coastal drainage: storm water outfall to Bay of Bengal
- Water body buffer zones: Gosthani River 30m buffer mandatory

## Water Supply
- WTP at Bheemunipatnam — supply from Gosthani River
- Pipeline distribution to coastal town wards
- Groundwater protection in bio-conservation zone

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM site designated — no coastal dumping mandate
- Fishing harbour area — dedicated waste collection for fish processing units

## Power and Streetlights
- HT Power lines along coastal corridor from Madhurawada substation
- Street lighting along NH coastal road (safety for fishing community)

## Health and Public Facilities
- Area Hospital at Bheemunipatnam (PSP zone)
- Fish Landing Sites designated (economic infrastructure)
- Police Station, Fire Station in PSP zone
- Heritage zone: Dutch Cemetery, historical structures protected

## Key Localities
Bheemunipatnam (Bheemili), Marripalem, Chinamushidivada, Yellapuvanipalem, Parawada, Desapatrunipalem, Revupolavaram, Akkireddipalem, Jaggayyapalem, Vedullapadu, Chintala-agraharam, Pudimadaka, Ravada, Chintapalli, Maradam, Bhogapuram

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage**: Gosthani River flood management, coastal drainage outfall
- **Roads**: NH coastal road and BRTS extension — critical for isolated coastal wards
- **Water**: Gosthani WTP supply to Bheemunipatnam town
- **Health**: Area Hospital PSP zone for coastal community
- **Garbage**: No-coastal-dumping zone — SWM enforcement
