# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Madhurawada Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA / GVMC
**Zone:** Madhurawada | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**GVMC Wards covered:** 15 (Seethammadhara Junction), 16 (HB Colony), 17 (Kotha Venkojipalem), 19 (MVP Sector-12), 27 (VIP Road), 29 (Kannayyapeta), 31 (Bheem Nagar), 36 (Agraharam Veedhi), 59 (Ashok Nagar Colony), 81 (Parts of GNT Road), 86 (Uppara Colony), 87 (Kurmannapalem Part), 88 (Narava), 89 (Chandra Nagar), 95 (MES Layout)
**Mandals:** Seethammadhara, Pendurthi, Maharanipeta, Anandapuram, Visakhapatnam Rural, Bheemunipatnam

## Land Use Zones
- Mixed Use Zones along GNT Road (National Highway coastal corridor)
- Proposed Residential zones R-2, R-3 (medium density) in Madhurawada, Kommadi, Yendada
- PSP Use Zones — IT park designation (Rushikonda IT SEZ), educational campuses
- Proposed Industrial zones on periphery (Bheemunipatnam fringe)
- Existing and Proposed Recreational Use Zones — Rushikonda Beach, Myraki Forest
- Green Zones buffer along coastal stretch
- Bhogapuram Airport & Influence Area (BAIA) Use Zone extends into northern reaches
- Water bodies: Mudasarlova lake, Gambheeram Reservoir — protected with buffer

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- NH (GNT Road / NH-16) — backbone arterial, proposed widening
- Existing BRTS Road on GNT Road + Proposed BRTS extensions northward
- Proposed Metro line extension: Bheemunipatnam branch
- Proposed Suburban Railway — Bheemunipatnam–Duvvada–city core link
- Proposed Elevated Coastal Corridor/road along Bay of Bengal
- State Highway connecting Madhurawada to Pendurthi

## Drainage and Water Management
- Sewage Treatment Plant at Bheemunipatnam (existing, capacity expansion proposed)
- Drainage system for Mudasarlova lake catchment — desilting and bund strengthening
- UGD planned for Madhurawada, Kommadi, Yendada wards
- Drainage outfall improvement to Bay of Bengal — coastal drain network

## Water Supply
- WTP at Mudasarlova — primary supply for Madhurawada zone wards
- Rising main pipeline from Yeleru augmentation scheme
- Service reservoir planned at Rushikonda heights for pressure zone management

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM transfer station at Madhurawada (near Auto Nagar fringe)
- IT park / commercial zone — enhanced collection frequency mandated
- Coastal zone littering controls — dedicated sweeping for beach areas

## Power and Streetlights
- 220/33 KV substation at Madhurawada (serves IT SEZ and residential zone)
- Street lighting: coastal corridor (Rushikonda–Bheemunipatnam) proposed solar street lights
- BRTS corridor — dedicated streetlighting for pedestrian safety

## Health and Public Facilities
- Hospitals in PSP zones: Bheemunipatnam Area Hospital, private hospital clusters along GNT Road
- GITAM Medical College, Seven Hills Hospital (PSP designation)
- Police Station, Fire Station in Madhurawada sector

## Education
- GITAM University campus (PSP zone)
- Andhra University Engineering campus fringe
- Schools in R-2 and R-3 residential sectors

## Key Localities
Madhurawada, Rushikonda (RUS), Kommadi, Yendada, Sontyam, Gudilova, Anandapuram, Kapuluppada, Nerallavalasa, Chepaluppada, Tatituru, Vellanki, Kothavalasa, Rajayyapeta, Gurrampalem, Juthada, Daddanem, Pedawaltair, Chinna Waltair

## Grievance Relevance
- **Drainage**: Mudasarlova catchment drainage, UGD for Madhurawada wards
- **Roads**: GNT Road widening, BRTS, Metro — key for wards 87–95
- **Water**: Mudasarlova WTP supply to medium-density wards
- **Streetlights**: Coastal corridor solar streetlights, BRTS-aligned lighting
- **Health**: PSP hospital zones at Bheemunipatnam and GNT Road clusters
- **Garbage**: Transfer station serving Madhurawada-Rushikonda corridor
