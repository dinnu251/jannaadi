This developmental plan blueprint, titled "DRAFT MASTER PLAN FOR VISAKHAPATNAM METROPOLITAN REGION (PROPOSED LANDUSE - 2041)", focuses on the Anandapuram Zone. The plan delineates proposed land use for the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region (VMR) until the year 2041, under the purview of VMRDA.

The geographical scope covers parts of Visakhapatnam District and Vizianagaram District. Key mandals depicted include Pendurthi, Kothavalasa, Lakkavarapukota, Padmanabh, Anandapuram, Visakhapatnam Rural, and Bheemunipatnam Mandals within Visakhapatnam District. From Vizianagaram District, Jami, Vizianagaram, Bhogapuram, and Denkada Mandals are shown. Specific localities and villages such as Tamarapalle, Chintalapalem, Rayapurajupeta, Nimmala, Mudapaka, Sowbhagya, Adivivaram, Balighattam, Uttarapalle, Ardhanapalem, Chinnipalem, Kantakapalle, Nidigattu, Katakapalle, Alamanda, Annamrajupeta, Kapuluppada, Agraharam Mokhasa, Korukonda, Nerallavalasa, Majjivalasa, Sangivalasa, Rajap, Cherikupalle, Mulakuddu, Polipalle, Chippada, Mopada, Godikomm, and D.Tallavalasa are explicitly mentioned or clearly depicted. The map also outlines the boundaries of Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation and Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation, along with village, T.S. Ward, T.S. Block, and Survey boundaries. The eastern boundary is defined by the Bay of Bengal.

Infrastructure scopes include an extensive network of existing and proposed transportation facilities. Existing infrastructure features National Highways, State Highways, Major District Roads, Other District Roads, Village Roads, Railway Lines, and HT Power lines. Proposed transportation networks include plans for Proposed BRTS (Bus Rapid Transit System), Proposed Metro, Proposed Suburban Railway, and a Proposed Elevated Coastal corridor/road. The plan also marks existing Cemeteries/Burial grounds, Religious Facilities, and Recreational Playgrounds. Key infrastructure projects highlighted include the Bhogapuram Airport (BALA), with its specific boundary indicated. The plan marks locations for Fish Landing Sites, Railway Stations, Sea Ports, and HT Pylon routes.

The proposed land use zones for 2041 categorize areas into Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Public & Semi-Public (PSP) Use, Recreational Use, Transport & Communication Use, and Protected Use zones, which include Forests, Hills, and Water bodies. Existing water bodies such as numerous lakes and reservoirs are depicted throughout the region. The plan also identifies existing Government layouts.

Key landmarks explicitly shown or detailed include the Bhogapuram Airport (BALA). The legend also indicates symbols for "Major Land Mark," "Junction," "Fish Landing Site," "HT Pylon," "Railway Station," and "Sea Ports" as general categories of landmarks. The overall project is a "DRAFT MASTER PLAN FOR VISAKHAPATNAM METROPOLITAN REGION" with a planning horizon extending to "2041," managed by the "VMRDA." The specific area highlighted in the blueprint is the "Anandapuram Zone."
