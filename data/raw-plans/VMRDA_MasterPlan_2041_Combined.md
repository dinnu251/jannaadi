# VMRDA Draft Master Plan 2041 — Combined Knowledge Base
**Authority:** Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA)
**Planning horizon:** 2041 | **Area:** Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region (VMR)
**Zones:** Gajuwaka · Visakhapatnam City · Madhurawada · Pendurthi · Bheemili · Anakapalli · Anandapuram · Vizianagaram Urban

---

## GRIEVANCE-TO-PLAN CROSS-REFERENCE

### Roads & Connectivity
The Master Plan proposes a multi-modal transport system across all 8 zones:
- **National Highways**: NH-16 (Visakhapatnam–Chennai) widening across all zones; NH-516 spur
- **BRTS (Bus Rapid Transit System)**: Existing BRTS Road in city core; Proposed BRTS extensions to Gajuwaka, Pendurthi, Anakapalli, Bheemunipatnam, Anandapuram, Vizianagaram
- **Metro Rail**: Proposed Metro lines — city core to Bheemunipatnam branch; northern extension to Anandapuram
- **Suburban Railway**: Bhogapuram–Duvvada–Duvvada–City–Anakapalli corridor
- **Elevated Corridors**: Proposed elevated roads for congestion relief in city and Madhurawada zones
- **Road Widening**: All arterial, sub-arterial, and major district roads within GVMC scheduled for widening
- **State Highways**: SH-23 (Anakapalli–Narsipatnam), SH-9 cross-city links
- **Village Roads**: Upgraded for rural mandal connectivity

### Drainage & Sewage
- **Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)**: Bheemunipatnam (existing + expansion), Gopalapatnam (existing + expansion), Pendurthi (proposed), Anandapuram (proposed), Gajuwaka zone (multiple sites), Anakapalli town, Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation
- **Underground Drainage (UGD)**: Planned citywide — all GVMC wards 1–98 in phased programme
- **Storm Water Drains**: Open drain cover-slabbing, desilting of Mudasarlova, Gambheeram Reservoir, Gosthani River catchment
- **Industrial Effluent**: Separate Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP) at Parawada SEZ — not mixed with municipal STP
- **River Management**: Gosthani River flood embankments; Nagavali River flood protection (Vizianagaram); coastal outfall upgrades

### Water Supply
- **Yeleru Augmentation Scheme**: Primary source for Visakhapatnam city wards — WTP at Mudasarlova
- **Gosthani River WTP**: Supply for Bheemunipatnam coastal zone
- **Mudasarlova WTP**: Madhurawada zone supply
- **Anakapalli WTP**: Yeleru scheme tributary supply for Anakapalli zone
- **Nagavali River WTP**: Vizianagaram Municipal Corporation supply
- **Anandapuram WTP**: Proposed from Yeleru extension
- **Jal Jeevan Mission**: Rural mandal pipeline coverage throughout VMR
- **Industrial Supply**: Dedicated pipeline for Parawada SEZ from Yerrakalava reservoir

### Solid Waste Management
- **Primary SWM Site**: Kapuluppada — serves entire city zone
- **Zone-level SWM**: Gajuwaka, Pendurthi, Madhurawada transfer stations
- **Anakapalli SWM**: Separate MSW site; Parawada hazardous waste facility
- **Anandapuram SWM**: Planned for growth node
- **Coastal Mandate**: No dumping within 200m of Bay of Bengal — Bheemili and Madhurawada zones
- **Airport Zone**: BALA airport SWM to AAI standards

### Power & Streetlights
- **220 KV Substations**: Madhurawada (IT SEZ), Gajuwaka (Steel Plant), Vizianagaram, Anakapalli Parawada
- **132 KV Substations**: Anakapalli town, Duvvada, Gopalapatnam
- **33/11 KV Distribution**: New substations at Pendurthi, Anandapuram, Bheemunipatnam
- **HT Power Lines**: Along NH-16, GNT Road coastal corridor, and all zone boundaries
- **Solar Streetlights**: Proposed for Rushikonda–Bheemunipatnam coastal corridor and BRTS corridors
- **Smart Lighting**: Proposed for Mixed Use Zone-2BA in city core

### Health Infrastructure
- **King George Hospital (KGH)**: City zone PSP — major referral, upgrades planned
- **Area Hospital Bheemunipatnam**: PSP zone, coastal community primary referral
- **Area Hospital Gopalapatnam**: PSP zone, serves Pendurthi zone wards 87–98
- **Area Hospital Vizianagaram**: Maharaja's Hospital teaching facility
- **Area Hospital Anakapalli**: PSP zone, proposed upgrade
- **PHC Pendurthi (Ward 96)**: Primary Health Centre in PSP zone
- **PHC per Mandal**: Community Health Centres in PSP zones across all mandals
- **GITAM Medical College**: Madhurawada zone PSP
- **Seven Hills Hospital**: GNT Road PSP designation

### Education
- **Andhra University**: Waltair, city zone PSP
- **GITAM University**: Madhurawada zone PSP
- **JNTU-GV Campus**: City zone PSP
- **Engineering Colleges**: Sabbavaram (Anakapalli zone), Vizianagaram (Vizianagaram zone)
- **Government Degree Colleges**: PSP zones in each zone
- **Schools**: PSP sub-zones per ward throughout all 8 zones

### Sanitation & Garbage Collection
- **SWMS at each zone**: SWM sites designated in all 8 zones
- **Transfer Stations**: Madhurawada, Gajuwaka, Pendurthi
- **Door-to-door collection**: Mandated in R-1 and Mixed Use Zone-2BA areas
- **Segregation at source**: Required in commercial C-1/C-2/C-3 zones

## KEY INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS (All Zones)

1. **Bhogapuram International Airport (BALA)** — under development, BAIA influence zone covers Anandapuram and Vizianagaram zones; new connectivity roads and logistics parks planned
2. **Visakhapatnam Metro Rail** — Phase 1 approved: Beach Road–Steel Factory; Phase 2 proposed: Bheemunipatnam branch, Anandapuram extension
3. **Suburban Railway** — Duvvada–Bhogapuram–Kothavalasa; city integration
4. **BRTS Network** — Existing on GNT Road; proposed extensions to Gajuwaka, Anakapalli, Pendurthi, Bheemunipatnam
5. **Elevated Coastal Corridor** — Madhurawada to Bheemunipatnam (tourism + congestion relief)
6. **Parawada SEZ (APSEZ)** — Major industrial zone; dedicated utilities, ETP, logistic parks
7. **Rushikonda IT SEZ** — Madhurawada zone; 220 KV substation, BRTS node
8. **Yeleru Augmentation Scheme** — Primary water supply backbone for city and surrounding zones
9. **Kapuluppada SWM** — Integrated solid waste processing for GVMC
10. **Gosthani River Management** — Flood embankment, estuary protection, Bheemili zone

## PLANNING NOTES FOR GVMC WARDS

- **Wards 73–80 (Gajuwaka/Pedagantyada)**: High industrial-residential conflict. Master plan designates buffer green zones between I-3 (Steel Plant/Parawada) and R-1 zones. STP and drainage critical — UGD Phase 2 priority.
- **Ward 75 (Pedagantyada)**: Master plan shows STP outfall route through this ward — drainage infrastructure targeted in 2024–2029 phased programme.
- **Ward 96 (Pendurthi)**: PHC designated in PSP zone. BRTS stop planned. Water overhead tank scheduled.
- **Wards 81–89 (Madhurawada corridor)**: NH-16/GNT Road widening directly affects these wards. Proposed Metro stop at Narava (Ward 88).
- **Wards 1–30 (City core)**: Aging infrastructure — water pipeline replacement, UGD Phase 1, road resurfacing under Mixed Use Zone-2BA development control.
