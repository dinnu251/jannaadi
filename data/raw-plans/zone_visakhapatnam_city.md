# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Visakhapatnam City Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA / Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (GVMC)
**Zone:** Visakhapatnam City | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**GVMC Wards covered:** 1–70 (core city wards including Bunglow Metta, Sabbivanipeta, Law College, MVP Colony, Seethammadhara, HB Colony, Madhura Nagar, VIP Road, Akkayyapalem, Port Quarters, Ashok Nagar, Gandhiji Street and all intervening wards)
**Mandals:** Maharanipeta, Seethammadhara, Visakhapatnam Rural, Gajuwaka, Pedagantyada, Pendurthi, Gopalapatnam, Anandapuram

## Land Use Zones
- Mixed Use Zone-2BA along major arterial corridors
- Residential zones (high to low density) — dense in central city, transitioning outward
- Industrial Use Zone around Gajuwaka fringe
- Proposed PSP (Public & Semi-Public) Use Zones for civic upgrades
- Proposed Recreational Use Zones — Myraki Forest, Madhurawada
- Waterfront and coastal zones along Bay of Bengal
- Defence areas maintained as protected use
- Vacant land pockets earmarked for future PSP and recreational development

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- National Highways: NH-16 (Vizag–Chennai) and NH-516 traversing city core
- BRTS Existing Road network + Proposed BRTS extensions
- Proposed Metro Rail lines through city core (Bheemunipatnam–Steel Factory corridor)
- Proposed Suburban Railway — connecting Duvvada, Pendurthi, Gopalapatnam to city
- Proposed Elevated Corridor/Flyovers for congestion relief
- Widening of existing arterial roads (sub-arterial and arterial grid)
- State Highways (SH-23, SH-9) for cross-city connectivity

## Drainage and Water Management
- Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) at Bheemunipatnam, Gopalapatnam (existing + proposed upgrade)
- Underground Drainage (UGD) expansion across city core wards
- Main drains along Sarada River, Gambheeram Reservoir catchment
- Drainage canal network — desilting and expansion proposed for wards 1–50
- Water body buffers: Mudasarlova tank, Kailasagiri, Gosthani River protected

## Water Supply
- Water Treatment Plants at Yeleru and Mudasarlova — supply backbone for city wards
- Distribution network upgrades across wards 1–72
- Pipeline replacement programme in aging ward areas (wards 1–30)
- Raised Storage Reservoirs (RSR) planned in high-density zones

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM sites designated at Kapuluppada (primary) and secondary sites at Mulagada
- Transfer stations planned per zone to serve wards 1–70
- Segregation at source mandated in Mixed Use Zone-2BA corridors

## Power and Streetlights
- HT Power substations: Gajuwaka, Gopalapatnam, Visakhapatnam Airport substations
- 33/11 KV distribution grid expansion for all city wards
- HT Pylon routes along coastal corridor
- Street lighting upgrades along NH-16, BRTS corridor, and all arterial roads

## Health and Public Facilities
- Major hospitals: King George Hospital (PSP zone), Area Hospital Gopalapatnam
- Visakhapatnam Airport (existing infrastructure, PSP use)
- Port Zone: Visakhapatnam Port (transport and commercial use)
- Police Stations, Fire Stations per sector in PSP zones
- Government offices, District Collectorate in PSP designation
- Dolphin Nose — tourism/landmark designation

## Education
- GITAM, Andhra University, JNTU campuses in PSP zones
- Schools per ward in PSP sub-zones
- Vocational training institutes planned in industrial fringe zones

## Key Localities
Gajuwaka, Mulagada, Dolphin Nose, Myraki Forest, Madhurawada, Rushikonda, Yarada, Gollalapalem, Pedagantyada, Narava, Mulagada, Perlupalem, Gopalapatnam, Gandigundam, Vepagunta, Sontyam, Kommadi, Yendada, Gudlavanipalem, Pothinamallayyapalem

## Grievance Relevance
- **Roads**: NH widening, BRTS, Metro, Elevated Corridor address congestion in central city wards
- **Drainage**: STP upgrades, UGD expansion directly address flooding in wards 1–50
- **Water**: WTP at Yeleru and Mudasarlova — supply gaps in dense wards 20–40
- **Garbage/Sanitation**: Kapuluppada SWM and zone transfer stations serve all city wards
- **Streetlights**: Substation grid expansion covers all 70 city wards
- **Health**: KGH and PSP hospital zones — major healthcare infrastructure
- **Education**: University/school PSP zones throughout city zone
