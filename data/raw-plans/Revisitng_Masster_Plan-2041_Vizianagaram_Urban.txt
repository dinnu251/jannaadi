This detailed blueprint outlines the DRAFT MASTER PLAN FOR VISAKHAPATNAM METROPOLITAN REGION (PROPOSED LANDUSE-2041), a comprehensive developmental map managed by VMRDA, incorporating the Vizianagaram Urban Zone. The plan delineates extensive geographic boundaries including the VMR Boundary, District Boundary, Mandal Boundary, Influence Area (BALA) Boundary, Village Boundary, Municipal Boundary, T.S. Ward Boundary, Block Boundary, and Survey Boundary. Key districts covered or bordering the region are Visakhapatnam District, Vizianagaram District, Anakapalli District, and Alluri Sitharama District, with the core urban cluster of Visakhapatnam city prominently depicted.

The infrastructure scope is broad, detailing various landuse zones. These include Residential areas segmented into R1, R2, R3, R4, and R5 categories, Commercial zones as C1, C2, and C3, and Industrial zones labeled I1, I2, and I3. Other designated land uses encompass Public & Semi Public (P&SP) zones, Open Space (OS), Agricultural (AGR) land, Water Body (WB) areas, Forest (F) zones, Ecologically Sensitive Zones (ESZ), Special Development Zones (SDZ), Hill Zones (HZ), and Transport & Communication (T&C) corridors. Environmental protections are integrated with Water Body Buffers, Green Zones, and Playgrounds. Specific symbols highlight Residential Layouts, Major Land parcels, Junctions, Fish Landing Sites, IT Pylons, Railway Stations, and Sea Ports.

Transportation infrastructure is a critical component, illustrating existing networks like National Highways, State Highways, Major District Roads, Other District Roads, Village Roads, and Railway Lines. The plan also shows HT Power lines and existing BRTS Roads. Future developments include Proposed BRTS routes and Proposed Roads, indicating a focus on enhancing connectivity. While specific budgets are not provided, the departmental label VMRDA signifies the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority as the overseeing body for this expansive development vision towards 2041. The plan details land use for a vast area, implicitly encompassing major Visakhapatnam localities and surrounding areas within its metropolitan region.
