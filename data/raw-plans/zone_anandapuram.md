# VMRDA Master Plan 2041 — Anandapuram Zone
**Authority:** VMRDA
**Zone:** Anandapuram | **Planning horizon:** 2041
**Districts:** Visakhapatnam District, Vizianagaram District
**Key Mandals:** Pendurthi, Kothavalasa, Lakkavarapukota, Padmanabh, Anandapuram, Visakhapatnam Rural, Bheemunipatnam, Jami, Vizianagaram, Bhogapuram, Denkada
**Key Areas:** Anandapuram, Kapuluppada, Bhogapuram Airport, Nidigattu, Chippada, Mopada

## Land Use Zones
- Residential zones — planned growth node for Anandapuram (R-2, R-3 medium density)
- Commercial zones along proposed BRTS and NH corridors
- Bhogapuram Airport & Influence Area (BAIA) Use Zone — aviation-related development, height restrictions
- Industrial zones at periphery (logistics near BALA airport)
- PSP Use Zones — civic facilities for growing residential population
- Recreational Use Zones — proposed parks and green corridors
- Green Zones along Gosthani River tributaries
- Agricultural and Protected Use zones in rural periphery
- Water bodies: lakes, reservoirs throughout mandal — protected with buffers
- Existing Government Layouts — regularized residential areas

## Roads and Transport Infrastructure
- NH connections: Anandapuram spur from NH-16
- Bhogapuram Airport (BALA) — approach roads and connectivity corridor
- Proposed BRTS: Bhogapuram–Anandapuram–Madhurawada–City corridor
- Proposed Metro: northern extension to Anandapuram from city
- Proposed Suburban Railway: Bhogapuram–Duvvada–City link
- State Highway: Anandapuram–Pendurthi link
- Village road network: Tamarapalle, Chintalapalem, Rayapurajupeta, Nimmala access
- HT Power lines and Pylon routes marked

## Drainage and Water Management
- STP planned for Anandapuram growth node
- Gosthani River tributary drainage management
- Lake-based drainage network — tanks maintained as recharge zones
- UGD planned for regularised government layouts

## Water Supply
- WTP planned for Anandapuram from Yeleru scheme extension
- Bhogapuram Airport dedicated water supply from nearest reservoir
- Village-level water supply: Jal Jeevan Mission pipeline coverage

## Solid Waste Management
- SWM facility planned for Anandapuram node (serves expanding residential population)
- Airport zone: dedicated waste management per AAI standards

## Power and Streetlights
- Power substation: Anandapuram (new, to serve growth node)
- Airport-area lighting: BALA influence zone lighting standards
- Streetlighting along NH spur and BRTS corridor

## Health and Public Facilities
- PHC and Community Health Centre in PSP zones
- Fish Landing Sites on Bheemunipatnam coast (Anandapuram zone fringe)
- Railway Stations: Duvvada (connectivity node)
- Religious Facilities and Cemeteries/Burial Grounds mapped

## Education
- Schools planned in PSP zones for Anandapuram residential growth
- BAIA zone: aviation training/institute potential

## Key Localities
Anandapuram, Kapuluppada, Nidigattu, Chippada, Mopada, Godikomm, D.Tallavalasa, Tamarapalle, Chintalapalem, Rayapurajupeta, Nimmala, Mudapaka, Sowbhagya, Adivivaram, Balighattam, Uttarapalle, Ardhanapalem, Chinnipalem, Kantakapalle, Katakapalle, Alamanda, Annamrajupeta, Cherikupalle, Mulakuddu, Polipalle

## Grievance Relevance
- **Roads**: BRTS, Metro, Suburban Railway for rapidly growing Anandapuram node
- **Drainage**: STP and lake-network drainage for growth node
- **Water**: WTP from Yeleru extension; Jal Jeevan coverage
- **Health**: PHC/CHC in PSP zones for expanding population
- **Garbage**: SWM planned for Anandapuram node
- **Streetlights**: New substation serving growth zone
