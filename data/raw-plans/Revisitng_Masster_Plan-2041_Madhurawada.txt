Proposed transportation enhancements feature the widening of existing roads, new Proposed BRTS routes, Proposed Suburban Railway lines, a Proposed Metro network, new Proposed Railway lines, and the development of an Elevated Corridor, which may include flyovers. The plan also delineates various Proposed Landuse Zones for 2041, categorized into different types of Residential Use Zones, Mixed Use Zones, Public Semi-Public Use Zones (PSP Use Zone), Industrial Use Zones, Proposed Industrial Use, Recreational Use Zones, and extensive Green Zones. Public Semi-Public Use Zones are designated for essential services, typically including facilities like schools, hospitals, water treatment plants, and other civic infrastructure. Areas are also earmarked for Bhogapuram Airport & Influence Area (BAIA) Use Zone, Proposed PSP Use Zone, and Proposed Recreational Use Zone. Natural features like Water Bodies, Forest, and Myraki Forest are conserved.

Key landmarks prominently shown or referenced include Madhurawada, which is the central focus area of this specific map, and the Rushikonda area, indicated by "RUS" near the coast, adjacent to significant green zones and proposed mixed-use developments. Other symbols indicate existing residential layouts, major landmarks, junctions, fish landing sites, railway stations, and sea ports, suggesting comprehensive planning for diverse urban and natural assets within the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region. The project, overseen likely by the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA), guides urban and regional planning until 2041.
