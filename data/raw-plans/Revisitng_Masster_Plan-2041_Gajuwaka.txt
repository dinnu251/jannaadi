The Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority (VMRDA) has drafted a comprehensive Master Plan, titled "Proposed Landuse - 2041", for the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region, outlining future development across Visakhapatnam District and Anakapalli District. The map delineates extensive geographic boundaries, encompassing numerous mandals and specific localities. Within the Anakapalli District, key areas include Anakapalli Mandal, Parawada Mandal, and Sabbavaram Mandal, with villages like Marripalem, Kurmannapalem, Desapatrunipalem, Gandigundam, Kondapeta, Vempadu, Chinnayapalem, Ravada, Pungavaka, Daggumilli, Ramavaram, Mamidilova, Vadlapudi, Akkireddipalem, Jaggayyapalem, Kothavalsa, Narsipatnam, Chinna Narsipatnam, Gantyada, and Kottapeta being part of the planned region.

The Visakhapatnam District section of the plan covers Pendurthi Mandal, Visakhapatnam Rural Mandal, Gajuwaka Mandal, Pedagantyada Mandal, and areas under the Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation. Notable localities and landmarks shown include Mulagada, Tallavalasa, Rushikonda, Kapulauppada, Yellapuvanipeta, Madhurawada, Yendada, Chepaluppada, Anandapuram, Marikavalasa, Lankelapalem, Vepagunta, and the coastal stretch along the Bay of Bengal. Other mentioned villages include Vangali, Chodavaram, Boni, Chinamushidivada, and Kanthigundam.

Infrastructure scopes are extensively detailed, with land use categories including various residential zones (R-1, R-2, R-3, R-4, R-5), commercial zones, industrial zones, public and semi-public use areas, open spaces, recreational zones, and designated water bodies. Special attention is given to agricultural use zones, forest areas, vacant land, and defence areas, in addition to waterfront development. The plan maps out existing and proposed transportation networks, including existing roads (major, arterial, sub-arterial, major district roads, other district roads, village roads) and proposed expressways and major roads. Railway lines, both existing and proposed, are clearly marked. Critical transport hubs like the existing Visakhapatnam Airport and existing and proposed ports are also indicated.

Essential utilities and services are integrated into the developmental blueprint. The plan identifies locations for Sewerage Treatment Plants (STP), Water Treatment Plants (WTP), and Solid Waste Management Sites (SWMS). Infrastructure for power is highlighted with proposed Power Substations. Drainage canals, main drains, water pipelines, and drainage pipelines are also part of the detailed planning. Public and social infrastructure includes locations for Educational Institutions, Hospitals/Healthcare facilities, Bus Terminals/Depots, Police Stations, Fire Stations, Government Offices, Commercial Centers, Markets, Religious Places, Petrol Pumps, Crematorium/Burial Grounds, Mobile Towers, and Police Outposts, alongside planned Parks/Gardens. Key landmarks explicitly depicted and central to the plan's geography are Madhurawada, Rushikonda, Gajuwaka, Pedagantyada, Anakapalli, and the Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation area, all situated within the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region aiming for development by 2041 under the VMRDA.
